Defense in Depth Architecture

A multi-layered approach to security ensures that if one mechanism fails, another steps up to thwart an attack.

1. Perimeter & Network Security

The first line of defense guarding the gates of your digital infrastructure.

Next-Gen Firewalls Intrusion Prevention Systems (IPS) Secure Web Gateways

2. Identity & Access Management (IAM)

Ensuring the right people have the right access at the right time. The "Zero Trust" core.

Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) Role-Based Access Control Single Sign-On (SSO)

3. Endpoint Security

Securing the devices (laptops, phones, servers) that connect to the network.

Endpoint Detection & Response (EDR) Mobile Device Management (MDM)

4. Data Security & Backups

Protecting the data itself and ensuring it can be recovered in worst-case scenarios.

End-to-End Encryption Data Loss Prevention (DLP) Immutable Backups
