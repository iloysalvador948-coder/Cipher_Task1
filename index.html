CipherTask Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Information We Collect

CipherTask collects your email address and name when you register or sign in via Google or Facebook. All todo data is encrypted on your device using AES-256-GCM encryption.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to authenticate your identity and associate your encrypted tasks with your account. We do not sell, share, or distribute your data to third parties.

Data Storage

All task data is stored locally on your device in encrypted form. We do not store your tasks on any external server.

Third-Party Services

We use Google Sign-In and Facebook Login for authentication. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for details on how they handle your data.
